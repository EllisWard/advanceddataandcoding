The State of Game Reviews

By Ellis Ward

Game reviews are an ever-changing entity, and the longer they have existed the more apparent their various flaws have become. Reviewers are too afraid to criticise games harshly and are often out of touch with what the wider audience likes and dislikes. This disparity can be shown by comparing the best-selling video games of 2017 to their average review score on console.
